What is Workout Wars?


Workout Wars is a workout competition for Mischief to get as fit as possible! While our team can't play frisbee together right now, we can still train and encourage each other to get stronger. The stronger and fitter you are, the less you're likely to be injured, and the more awesome we will be once we can all play together again! It is a competition individually, as well as pod vs. pod. Grab your teammates (at a distance), gear up, and get ready to battle!

What You Do:


1. Workout
2. Log your workouts & get points
3. Check the scoreboard to see who is crushing!!

Workouts and Points


Each workout has a different point value based on intensity and/or skills we want to prioritize for the season. Total points for an entry = Workout point level * multiplier (increment varies depending on workout). Only log time based on how long you actually did the exercise. For example, if you play basketball for an hour but sub out for half of the points, you should only log 30 minutes.
