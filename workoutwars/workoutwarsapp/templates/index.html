WORKOUT WARS 2.0: SPRING 2020 EDITION


Our season was unfortunately cut short due to COVID-19, but that doesn't mean we need to stop working out! In order to help us stay connected as a program AND stay in shape, we are bringing back Workout Wars this spring! IMPORTANT: Safety is our number one priority! This means that for team sports, sub out playing the sport with the solo version. This could mean dribbling a basketball, hitting a tennis ball against a wall, etc. We've also removed some activities such as throwing and playing frisbee to protect our health. Can't wait to see everyone's selfies to help get through this together!


What is Workout Wars?


Workout Wars is the annual Pie Queen competition of getting as fit/swole/yoked as possible over winter break. The stronger and fitter you are, the less you're likely to be injured, and the more awesome we will be this spring! It is a competition individually, class vs. class (this includes the coaches!) and as one of two workout wars teams. Grab your teammates, gear up, and get ready to battle. Watch this video from a couple years ago to get hyped!

What You Do:


1. Workout
2. Log your workouts & get points
3. Check the scoreboard to see who is crushing!!

Winners!


We'll celebrate winning team, winning class, and winning individual! There's a lot of bragging rights to be had and also some seriously swag-arific prizes from our coaches. Friendly heckling/smack talk is a thing.

Workouts and Points


Each workout has a different point value based on intensity and/or skills we want to prioritize for the season. Total points for an entry = Workout point level * multiplier (increment varies depending on workout). Only log time based on how long you actually did the exercise. For example, if you play basketball for an hour but sub out for half of the points, you should only log 30 minutes.
